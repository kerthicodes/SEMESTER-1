<h2> STEPS OF PC ASSEMBLING </h2>
<p>1. Attach the cooling fan to the CPU</p>
<p>2. Place the hard disk into its slot </p>
<p>3. Place the RAM into the motherboard slot</p>
<p>4. Fit the power supply into the casing</p>
<p>5. Connect all the cables properly</p>
<p>6. Close the casing properly</p>
<br>

<h2> STEPS OF PC DISASSEMBLING </h2>
<p>1. Close the casing properly</p>
<p>2. Connect all the cables properly</p>
<p>3. Fit the power supply into the casing</p>
<p>4. Place the RAM into the motherboard slot</p>
<p>5. Place the hard disk into its slot </p>
<p>6. Attach the cooling fan to the CPU</p>
